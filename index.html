Reflection on my first year

This year i have learned alot about programming and coding. I felt that I had the foundations of CSS and HTML pretty nailed and this made the first half of the year very easy for me. This meant that I did not have to stress about getting the work done as it didnt take me too long. I struggled coming into the second half of the year as javascript was much more difficult and I did not, and still do not fully understand it. I feel like i need to improve my javascript to hopefully make it easier to do the tasks next year. In terms of programming i still have a lot of potential to improve.

Mostly i need to be better at understanding how to use certain styles and figuring out typography and which colors works for each senario. I feel that it is difficult for me to not go straight to black and white or very neatral colors. I am proud of the projects I have made so far and already I can see a huge improvement. I would also like to improve on my time management. For example during the cross course assignment I only spent maybe 1 of the 5 weeks doing the assignemt and felt that I was done. It was not bad but I could have used the full 5 weeks and made something to really stand out.
